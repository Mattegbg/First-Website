Mike Tyson





One of the best fighters of all time ''Iron Mike''

Michael Gerard "Mike" Tyson, nicknamed "Iron Mike", born June 30, 1966 in Brooklyn, New York, is an American former heavyweight boxer, known as the youngest heavyweight world champion ever, holder of World Cup titles 1986-1990 and 1996.

Short history of boxing

Boxing is a martial art where two people compete in strength, speed, reflexes (timing and emotion), perseverance and will by hitting each other with special padded gloves inside a square raised ring. The sport is monitored by a judge in 4-12 rounds that lasts three minutes. The result is decided when one of the competitors is judged to be unable to continue, is disqualified, gives up or is named the winner by a panel of judges after the match.

Ingemar ''Ingo'' Johansson





Swedish boxing legend and former world champion ''Ingo''

Jens Ingemar "Ingo" Johansson, originally Ingemar Johansson, born September 22, 1932 in Gothenburg, died January 30, 2009 in Onsala, was a Swedish amateur and later professional boxer who won Olympic silver in 1952 and became professional world champion in 1959.
